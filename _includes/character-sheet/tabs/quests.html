Monthly draw and dice helpers

Sources that affect establishing your monthly quest pool or related dice rolls (equipped items, passive display or familiar slots, not inventory-only). Mark uses that reset at End of Month (same pattern as Worn Page helpers on the Curses tab).

When drawing quest cards below, turning on Auto-use helpers on draw will automatically use the first available monthly helper for that deck (one helper per click; no stacking). Turn it off to draw one card per click and mark helpers used manually.

With Auto-use helpers on draw, Divination is marked used when you click any quest pool deck — ♥ genre, ♣ side, ♠ dungeon rooms, or ⭐ Extra Credit (roll 2 dice for that pick; no extra cards drawn). You can still mark it manually if auto is off.

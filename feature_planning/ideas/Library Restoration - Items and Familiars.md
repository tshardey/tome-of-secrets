# Library Restoration - Passive Items & Familiars Ideas

These are ideas for passive items and familiars that could be rewarded from restoration projects, dungeon room encounters, side quests, or wing completions in the Library Restoration expansion.

**Reward Sources:**
- Restoration project completion (unlocks passive slots)
- Dungeon room encounters (befriend/defeat rewards)
- Side quest rewards
- Wing completion bonuses
- Special events

## Notes on Balance

**Passive Slot Bonuses** should be roughly half of active/wearable bonuses:
- Active: +8-10 Ink Drops → Passive: +4-5 Ink Drops
- Active: +15-20 Ink Drops → Passive: +7-10 Ink Drops
- Active: x2 multiplier → Passive: x1.5 multiplier
- Active: x3 multiplier → Passive: x1.5 multiplier

**Utility Items** (atmospheric buffs, rerolls, etc.) should have weaker passive versions:
- Active: Full atmospheric buff (x2) → Passive: Always-on but smaller (+2-3 Ink Drops)
- Active: Once per month ability → Passive: Once per 2 months or weaker version

**Always-Active Bonuses** (Wing Completion Rewards):
- Should be permanent and not require slots
- Should stack with equipped/passive items
- Should be moderate (+3-5 Ink Drops for genre categories)

## Reward Distribution Guidelines

### Restoration Projects
- Unlock passive item or familiar slots
- Players choose which items/familiars to place in unlocked slots

### Dungeon Room Encounters
- **Befriending Creatures**: May reward specific items or familiars
- **Defeating Monsters**: Standard XP rewards
- **Room Completion**: Standard book completion rewards

### Side Quests
- May reward unique items or familiars
- Can provide special utility items

### Wing Completion
- Always provides a special unique reward
- Examples: Drafting Compass (Wing 6), Raven Familiar (Wing 5)

# Genre Quest Image Generation Prompts (Vintage National Park Style)

## Color Palette

Use this palette for all images. These colors are chosen to evoke the "Vintage National Park Poster" aesthetic while distinguishing Genre Quests from Side Quests. This palette leans into cooler, more ethereal tones suitable for abstract genre representations, while maintaining the "Dark Academia" grounding.

*   **Twilight Violet:** `#2C2B3C` (Deep mystery, shadow)
*   **Sage Green:** `#556B2F` (Nature, groundedness)
*   **Dusty Rose:** `#BC8F8F` (Romance, softness)
*   **Steel Blue:** `#4682B4` (Sci-fi, clarity)
*   **Amber Gold:** `#FFBF00` (Highlights, warmth)
*   **Charcoal Grey:** `#36454F` (Gritty realism, noir)

---

## Image Prompts

All prompts follow the requested style: Vintage National Park / NASA poster aesthetic, minimal outlining, bold colors, 1:1 aspect ratio, no people/creatures, fantasy setting.

### 1. Historical Fiction
**Prompt:**
> An image of a scene in the style of vintage national park posters or like the vintage nasa posters. It should have minimal outlining, emphasizing broad areas of bold colors. It should be a 1:1 aspect ratio. It should not have any people or creatures in it. The scene is: A lavish Art Deco mansion facade lit up at night, reminiscent of the Great Gatsby era. It sits at the edge of a dark bay. The lights reflect in the water in shimmering bands of Amber Gold and Dusty Rose against the Twilight Violet sky. Fantasy setting.

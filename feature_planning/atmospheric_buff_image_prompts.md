# Atmospheric Buff Image Generation Prompts (Vintage National Park Style)

## Color Palette

Use this palette for all images. These colors are chosen to evoke a "Cozy Hearth & Home" aesthetic, distinguishing them from the Quest decks. The focus is on warmth, comfort, and the inviting nature of a reading space.

*   **Hearthside Red:** `#8B0000` (Deep, warm red, fire)
*   **Golden Ember:** `#FFB347` (Glowing orange, candlelight)
*   **Parchment Cream:** `#F5F5DC` (Soft light, pages)
*   **Library Oak:** `#654321` (Dark wood, furniture)
*   **Nightshade Blue:** `#2C3E50` (Contrast, evening sky)
*   **Sage Velvet:** `#556B2F` (Upholstery, plants)

---

## Image Prompts

All prompts follow the requested style: Vintage National Park / NASA poster aesthetic, minimal outlining, bold colors, 1:1 aspect ratio, no people/creatures, fantasy setting.

### 1. The Candlight Study
**Buff:** Light a [scented] candle while you read.
**Prompt:**
> An image of a scene in the style of vintage national park posters or like the vintage nasa posters. It should have minimal outlining, emphasizing broad areas of bold colors. It should be a 1:1 aspect ratio. It should not have any people or creatures in it. The scene is: A close-up of a sturdy Library Oak desk in a darkened room. The centerpiece is a large, melting pillar candle emitting a warm Golden Ember glow. Beside it lies an open book with Parchment Cream pages. Shadows in Nightshade Blue dance on the walls. Fantasy setting.

# Side Quest Image Generation Prompts (Vintage National Park Style)

## Color Palette

Use this palette for all images. These colors are chosen to evoke the "Vintage National Park Poster" aesthetic while maintaining a connection to the muted, fantasy tones of the game.

*   **Midnight Teal:** `#1B4332` (Deep, forest shadow)
*   **Rust Orange:** `#9D4928` (Aged warmth, clay)
*   **Slate Indigo:** `#34495E` (Night sky, mystery)
*   **Muted Gold:** `#C6A664` (Highlights, magic)
*   **Warm Sand:** `#DBC1AC` (Parchment, light areas)
*   **Deep Maroon:** `#58181F` (Rich accents)

## Image Prompts

### 1. The Arcane Grimoire
**Prompt:**
> An image of a scene in the style of vintage national park posters or like the vintage nasa posters. It should have minimal outlining, emphasizing broad areas of bold colors. It should be a 2:3 aspect ratio. It should not have any people or creatures in it. The room is: An ancient wizard's study dominated by a large mahogany desk. On the desk sits an open, glowing grimoire. Surrounding the desk are towering bookshelves filled with leather-bound volumes. The lighting casts deep shadows in Midnight Teal and Slate Indigo, while the book glows with Muted Gold. Fantasy setting.
